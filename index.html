Tarun Rambha

 

Hi! I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate in the School of Civil & Environmental Engineering at Cornell University. I work with Dr. Linda Nozick and my research primarily focusses on optimization of transportation networks. Before joining Cornell, I received my PhD from The Department of Civil, Environmental & Architectural Engineering at The University of Texas at Austin.

A copy of my CV can be found here.

Research Interests

Real-time Control of Traffic Networks

Dynamic congestion pricing for self-driving vehicles
Stochastic shortest paths
Smart parking models

Network Optimization

Static and dynamic traffic equilibrium
Signal control algorithms

Large Scale Evacuation Modeling

Hurricane evacuation
Evacuation of pedestrian crowds

Shared Ride and Transit Systems

Online vehicle routing and scheduling
Pricing and matching for ride-hailing and ride-sharing
